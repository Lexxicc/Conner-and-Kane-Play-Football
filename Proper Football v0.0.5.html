How to Play

Tap a position on the pitch to open the player picker. Choose one of three cards from your squad pool.

Fill all five positions, then tap KICK OFF to play out the sequence.

POSITIONS

• Cross-In Left — Left foot + accuracy

• Through Ball Run [chain] — Speed + control. Only scores if the cross is received.

• Pen Box Edge Left — Left foot + accuracy

• Straight Shot — Accuracy + power

• Long Shot Right — Power + right foot

CROSS CHAIN

Cross-In Left fires first. Reception depends on the crosser's quality and the runner's control. If the ball isn't received, Through Ball Run scores zero.

CHALLENGE

Share your challenge code after the game. Opponents face the exact same squad pool. Three minutes on the clock — timer starts on your first tap.
