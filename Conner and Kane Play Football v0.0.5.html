How to Play

Pick players from your squad pool and assign them to positions.

Tap a player card to select it. Tap an empty slot to assign. Tap a filled slot to remove.

POSITIONS

• Pen Box Edge Left — Left foot + accuracy

• Long Shot Right — Power + right foot

• Straight Shot — Accuracy + power

• Cross-In Left [chain] — Left foot + accuracy

• Through Ball Run [chain] — Speed + control

CROSS CHAIN

Cross-In Left fires first. If the ball is received, the Through Ball Run scores. If not, it scores zero. Reception depends on the crosser's quality and the runner's control. The outcome is shown live once both slots are filled.

CHALLENGE

Share your challenge code after the game. Opponents face the exact same squad pool and cross chain outcome. Three minutes on the clock — timer starts on your first move.
